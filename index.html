Additional information about me

I'd like to share with you little story. How I came to the point that programming is interesting for me. When I was a child I used to record old comedy shows from TV to video tapes. In order to do that I studied instructions of video player, to see what kind of features it has. So while reading instructions, I came to that part where it says you may record TV channels, if you will follow certain rules. So I read it, however for the first time I wasnt't brave enough to change something on video player and TV itself. I was thinking and imagining how would I record TV channel, without damaging anything. Because when you are a child you know that if you ruin something you're going to get punished for that. So finally I decided to give it a try and recorded first comedian TV show. Then after several months. I showed recorded TV show to my parents and guests who came at that time to our house. I know it's not related to real world programming, but for me it was a big challenge and I overcame that. This is a short story happened 20 years ago. When I used to be a school boy. When I was a child I used to be very good at technical staff and reading some kind of instructions. Now I think that my child behavior returned to me again, returned to me to try out programming. I hope I can get all the plans realized. I'm really trying my best to get there!
